# Reforms to securitisation requirements

This folder contains the consultation [CP2/26 – Reforms to securitisation requirements](https://www.bankofengland.co.uk/prudential-regulation/publication/2026/february/reforms-to-securitisation-requirements-consultation-paper) (printed as a PDF) and the five PDF appendices. 

Responses to the consultation can be added by searching for [`response to CP2/26 - Reforms to securitisation requirements filetype:pdf`](https://duckduckgo.com/?q=response+to+CP2%2F26+-+Reforms+to+securitisation+requirements+filetype%3Apdf&ia=web)

You could also add: 

* [Regulation (EU) 2017/2402](https://eur-lex.europa.eu/legal-content/EN/TXT/PDF/?uri=CELEX:32017R2402) "laying down a general framework for securitisation and creating a specific framework for simple, transparent and standardised securitisation". Article 6 (risk retention) and Article 7 (transparency/disclosure) are the two most relevant and most self-contained for summaries focused on Collateralised Loan Obligations (CLOs).
* A rating agency's public CLO methodology (S&P or Moody's)
* The EBA/ESMA/EIOPA Joint Committee reports for [Spring 2025](https://www.esma.europa.eu/sites/default/files/2025-03/Joint_Committee_Update_on_risks_and_vulnerabilities_in_the_EU_financial_system_-_Spring_2025.pdf), [Autumn 2025](https://www.eiopa.europa.eu/document/download/6ad57270-4874-40aa-98bc-a562f170bb59_en?filename=Joint%20Committee%20Report%20on%20risks%20and%20vulnerabilities%20in%20the%20EU%20financial%20system%20-%20Autumn%202025.pdf) and [2026](https://www.eiopa.europa.eu/document/download/d7236072-baf4-4460-b575-9c646dd760d4_en?filename=Joint%20Committee%20Update%20on%20risks%20and%20vulnerabilities%20in%20the%20EU%20financial%20system%20-%20Spring%202026.pdf)
