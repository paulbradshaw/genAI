# Gender pay gap action plans

The [gender pay gap service](https://gender-pay-gap.service.gov.uk/) refers to "Action plans show the steps employers are taking to address their gender pay gap and support employees experiencing menopause" and links to [guidance](https://www.gov.uk/government/publications/creating-an-action-plan-guidance-for-employers) on creating that, including a collection of "[evidence-informed actions to reduce your gender pay gap and support employees experiencing menopause](https://www.gov.uk/government/collections/action-plans-list-of-actions)".

[Searching for "gender pay gap action plan"](https://duckduckgo.com/?q=gender+pay+gap+action+plan&ia=web) identifies documents by a wide range of companies. Some of those are in this folder.
