# Cognitive bias and interviews

Bias isn't just an AI problem - it's a human problem too. Here's a prompt to help systematically identify how cognitive biases might affect an interview, and take steps to address those.

```
I am interviewing [DESCRIBE INTERVIEWEE] for a story on ___.
I am interviewing them because I believe they will bring ____ to the story.
I am expecting them to say ___.

Create a table of potential cognitive biases that may come into play during the interview (for me or the interviewee).
Include a column of succinct practical steps to take to avoid or manage those. 
```
